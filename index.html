Get FindSpot on your Phone.

FindSpot is a Progressive Web App (PWA). No App Store required—just install directly from your browser for full offline capability.

Android

  • 1Open findspot.uk in Chrome.
  • 2Tap the menu icon (⋮) or look for the "Install" banner.
  • 3Select "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen."

iPhone & iPad

  • 1Open findspot.uk in Safari.
  • 2Tap the "Share" icon (square with an up arrow).
  • 3Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen."

★ Tip: Once installed, FindSpot works perfectly offline in the field.
